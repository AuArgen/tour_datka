About This Tour

Discover Burana Tower and Konorchek Canyons

The most amazing sights of the Chui valley in one day! The ancient Burana Tower is one of Kyrgyzstan's most outstanding historical sites, and the famous Konorchek Canyons — formed by red sandy rocks eroded by rain and wind — are equally breathtaking.

With Datka Travel you travel privately — your own guide, your own vehicle, your own pace. We've pre-tested every tour and only work with the finest local guides who know these landscapes deeply.

11th-century Burana Tower & balbals

Step by Step

What to Expect

1
Burana Tower & Archaeological Site
Begin at the iconic Burana Tower — climb the 11th-century minaret, walk the balbal field and explore the on-site museum.
2
Picnic Lunch
A picnic-style lunch with homemade dishes, enjoyed in the Chuy Valley countryside.
3
Drive to Konorchek Canyons
Continue into the rugged landscape of the Konorchek Canyon system — a geological wonder of red sandstone formations.
4
Canyon Hike
Navigate the canyon labyrinth on foot — narrow passages, towering walls of ancient rock, views like another planet.
5
Summit Viewpoint
Climb to the canyon rim for sweeping views across the Chuy Valley to the mountains above Bishkek.
6
Return to Bishkek
Drive back as the sun paints the red rocks golden. Hotel drop-off around 7 PM.
