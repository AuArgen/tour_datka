About This Tour

Discover One Nomad Day: Horseback Riding and Archery

One of our favourite trips — a full nomadic adventure in the Chunkurchak gorge. Visit the Pigeon waterfall, cross the suspension bridge, do horseback riding, complete an archery masterclass and enjoy a scenic picnic. The quintessential Kyrgyz nomad experience.

With Datka Travel you travel privately — your own guide, your own vehicle, your own pace. We've pre-tested every tour and only work with the finest local guides who know these landscapes deeply.

Step by Step

What to Expect

1
Pigeon Waterfall
Hike to the beautiful Pigeon waterfall in the Chunkurchak gorge — a dramatic cascade fed by mountain streams.
2
Suspension Bridge
Cross the heart-pumping suspension bridge over the gorge river — a highlight that gets the blood pumping.
3
Horseback Riding
Mount your horse for a scenic ride through the gorge — meadows, river crossings and forest trails. Beginners are fully guided.
4
Archery Masterclass
Learn the ancient nomadic art of archery with a traditional Kyrgyz bow. Surprisingly challenging and deeply satisfying.
5
Nomadic Games & Activities
Try traditional nomadic games — kok-boru polo demonstration, kurash wrestling, and the traditional eagle whistle.
6
Picnic Lunch with Mountain Views
A beautifully arranged Kyrgyz picnic with views across the gorge — fresh bread, cheese, meat dishes and mountain spring water.
