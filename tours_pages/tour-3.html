About This Experience

Genuine Kyrgyz Hospitality in a Local Home

This is not a restaurant — it is a real home, a real family, and a truly authentic evening. You'll be welcomed with the warmth that defines Kyrgyz culture: tea, conversation, and food prepared together.

Help prepare traditional dishes including boorsok (fried dough), and learn the secrets passed down through generations. Your guide translates stories about nomadic customs, seasonal traditions, and daily Kyrgyz life.

Share a meal at the family's dastarkhan (tablecloth spread on the floor) and engage in genuine cultural exchange. A memorable dive into authentic living, perfect for cultural explorers seeking connection over cuisine.

The Evening Program

Your Experience

1
Arrival & Welcome Tea
Your guide picks you up and takes you to a traditional Bishkek home. You're greeted with Kyrgyz green tea, kaimak (cream), and light snacks — a centuries-old expression of hospitality.
2
Cooking Together
Apron on — you're joining the kitchen! Learn to shape manti dumplings, fry boorsok dough, and stir a simmering shorpo. The family shares techniques and stories while you cook.
3
The Dastarkhan Dinner
Gather around the dastarkhan for a full spread of Kyrgyz home cooking — everything you helped prepare, plus the host's special family recipes. Eat, laugh, and share stories.
4
Cultural Exchange
After dinner, enjoy tea with homemade jam and sweets. Your guide facilitates a genuine exchange — family customs, nomadic heritage, Kyrgyz music, and the meaning of their traditional crafts.
5
Farewell & Return
Leave with full hearts, recipes written down, and a connection that transcends tourism. Dropped off at your hotel with memories that will last a lifetime.
