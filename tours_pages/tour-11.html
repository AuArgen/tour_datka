About This Tour

Discover Horseback Riding in Chon Kemin National Park

The guide will introduce you to the history of the Silk-Road-era Burana Tower, then take you on a horseback ride through the scenic Chon-Kemin National Park — a pristine alpine valley that feels worlds away from any city. Suitable for all riding levels.

With Datka Travel you travel privately — your own guide, your own vehicle, your own pace. We've pre-tested every tour and only work with the finest local guides who know these landscapes deeply.

11th-century Burana Tower & balbals

Step by Step

What to Expect

1
Burana Tower Visit
Morning at the archaeological Burana Tower — ascend the minaret, explore the balbal field, and hear about the ancient Karakhanid dynasty.
2
Drive to Chon Kemin Valley (1 hr)
Head east into the pristine Chon Kemin valley — a long, green corridor between forested mountains, almost completely free of tourism.
3
Meet Your Horse
Introduction to your horse, saddle fitting and a brief riding lesson for beginners. Experienced riders join the longer trail directly.
4
Horseback Trail Ride (3 hrs)
Ride through meadows, along mountain rivers and through alpine forest. Your guide shares nomadic horse culture — the heart of Kyrgyz identity.
5
Traditional Lunch
A hearty meal with a local family — beshbarmak, dairy products, and fresh bread.
6
Return to Bishkek
Drive back through the golden Chon Kemin valley. Hotel drop-off around 7 PM.
