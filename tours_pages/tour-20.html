About This Tour

Discover Best of Song Kul and Issyk Kul Lakes Tour in 4 Days

Explore Song Kul & Issyk Kul Lakes, each offering its own unique experience. Discover nomadic life and untouched nature at Song Kul, and embrace adventure among Issyk Kul's mountains and valleys.

With Datka Travel you travel privately — your own guide, your own vehicle, your own pace. We've pre-tested every tour and only work with the finest local guides who know these landscapes deeply.

Tour Highlights
Song-Kul alpine lake (3,016 m)
Yurt night at Song-Kul
Issyk-Kul lake adventures
Skazka Canyon & petroglyphs
Horseback riding at both lakes
Cultural immersion with nomads
Step by Step

What to Expect

1
Day 1 — Bishkek to Song-Kul
Drive via the Kalmak-Ashu Pass to the sacred Song-Kul Lake. Horseback ride, yurt accommodation, sunset over the steppe.
2
Day 2 — Song-Kul to Issyk-Kul
Morning at Song-Kul before the drive east and descent to the Issyk-Kul basin. Check into lakeside accommodation.
3
Day 3 — Issyk-Kul Exploration
Skazka Canyon, Konorchek Canyon, and Rukh Ordo cultural complex. Evening boat ride on the lake.
4
Day 4 — Return to Bishkek
Morning at the lake, then the scenic Boom Gorge drive back to Bishkek.
